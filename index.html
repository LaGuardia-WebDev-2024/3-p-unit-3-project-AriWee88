Recipe #1

Super Easy
Microwaved Korean Steamed Egg

Ingredients Quantity
  • eggs
  • sesame oil
  • salt
  • water
  • scallions(green onion)

  • 3-4 eggs

  • drizzle of sesame oil!

  • Pinch of salt

  • Amount of water explained later

  • Chop up 2 stems of scallions

    		•

    Step 1:

    1. crack the amounts of eggs you want into a bowl, if you have a korean stone bowl, use that!
    2. Next, whisk the eggs as much as you can and add a bit less than half of the mixed eggs.
    3. To this new mixture, add a drizzle of sesame oil and a pinch of salt
    4. You can shoose to add sesame seeds and/or scallions into the bowl before or after putting it in the microwave. Both can serve as a garnish as well.
    5. Make sure to mix it well.
    6. Put it in the microwave for 1 min 30 sec.
    7. Once the time is up check to see if it's ready by poking a hole in it. if it's too spongey it may be overcooked but that's also a result from cooking it quick in the microwave.
    8. If it's not ready, add another 15 secs and check it until it's done
    9. Finally, you can add the final touches, scallions and sesame seeds.
      10. You can also serve it as a side dish or with rice!!
